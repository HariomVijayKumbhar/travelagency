Popular Destinations

Explore the world's most beautiful destinations with our carefully crafted tour packages

MUNNAR Hill Station

Munnar is a hill station located in the Idukki district of Kerala, known for its misty valleys, sprawling tea plantations, and biodiversity. Visitors can see natural attractions like Eravikulam National Park, Anamudi Peak (the highest peak in South India), and various waterfalls and dams like Mattupetty and Attukal.

GOA BEACHES

Goa's coast is divided into North Goa, known for lively, crowded beaches, and South Goa, famous for its peaceful and serene shores. Here is a look at some of the most prominent beaches

MUMBAI Cities

Mumbai is located on the west coast of India, situated on the Konkan coast, and is known as India's financial and commercial center. Visitors can see many sights,including the Gateway of India, Juhu Beach, and the historic Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (formerly Victoria Terminus).

RAIGAD Fort

Raigad is a district in Maharashtra's Konkan region, located alongthe Arabian Sea coast. You can see remnants ofpalaces, a royal mint, and a Samadhi of Shivaji Maharaj at the fort,as well as other sites like the Janjira Fort and the beaches of Alibag and Kashid.

DENDELI Adventure

Dandeli is a town in the Uttara Kannada district of Karnataka, India,located in the Western Ghats. Visitors can see lush forests, wildlife,and adventure activities, with attractions including the Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary, Kavala Caves, and Sathodi Falls. The KaliRiver is central to many activities, which include white water rafting, kayaking, and trekking.

RISHIKESH Temple

Rishikesh is a city in the Uttarakhand Himalayas, situated at the confluence of the Ganga and Chandrabhaga rivers. You can see numerous temples, such as the ancient Bharat Mandir, the 13-storied Trayambakeshwar Temple, and the Neelkanth Mahadev Temple. Visitors can also explore ashrams, walk across suspension bridges like Lakshman Jhula, and experience the spiritual atmosphere of the region,
