About Dandeli

Dandeli is a town in the North Western part of Karnataka known for its lush green forests, wildlife, and adventure activities.

It's a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers offering white-water rafting, trekking, bird watching, and kayaking amidst serene surroundings of the Western Ghats.

  • Wildlife Sanctuary: Diverse fauna and flora
  • White-water Rafting: Thrilling river rapids
  • Kayaking & Canoeing: Explore rivers
  • Trekking: Scenic trails
  • Bird Watching: Rich avian diversity
  • Syntheri Rocks: Natural granite formations
