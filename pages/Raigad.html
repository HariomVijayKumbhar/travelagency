About Raigad

Raigad is a hill fort located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India. It was the capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and is known for its historical significance and stunning views.

Raigad Fort offers a glimpse into India's rich history. Visitors can explore the fort's architecture, museums, and enjoy trekking to the top for breathtaking views of the surrounding Sahyadri mountains.

  • Historical Significance: Capital of Maratha Empire
  • Fort Architecture: Ancient Maratha construction
  • Museum: Artifacts from Shivaji's era
  • Trekking: Challenging hike to the fort
  • Panoramic Views: Sahyadri mountain range
  • Water Tanks: Ancient water management system
